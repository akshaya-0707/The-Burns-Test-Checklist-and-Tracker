# Burns Monday Check-In App

This tool brings the Burns Depression Checklist into a simple, repeatable weekly ritual. Every Monday, you work through the same set of questions covering mood, motivation, self-esteem, physical symptoms, and other key indicators tied to depressive symptoms. Instead of just answering once and forgetting about it, your responses are saved and scored automatically, so you can watch how your numbers shift from week to week.

The interface is built for quick, low-friction check-ins: a clean checklist, a clear scoring summary, and a visual history view that shows your trend over time rather than just a single snapshot.

It is designed as a personal self-awareness tool, not a diagnostic instrument.

A simple web app to complete the Burns Depression Checklist weekly (recommended on Monday), calculate your score instantly, and track your progress over time.

## Features

- Full 25-item Burns checklist
- 0 to 4 rating per item
- Automatic total score and severity label
- Weekly save by Monday week start (one saved entry per week, auto-updated)
- Local progress chart and entry history
- Safety notice when suicidal-distress items are above 0

## How to run

Open `index.html` in your browser.

No installation is required.

## Notes

- Data is stored in your browser (`localStorage`) on this device only.
- This app is for self-tracking and is not a medical diagnosis tool.
- If you are in immediate danger or might harm yourself, call local emergency services right away.
